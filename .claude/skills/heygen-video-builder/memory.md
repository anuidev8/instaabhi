### Key Decision: NO CHARACTER IN APP PROMOS
The user explicitly confirmed: app promotional videos must use ONLY real app screenshots — NO Abhi avatar/character/presenter on screen. The character is reserved for personal-story content (Instagram carousel with character style). App promos = pure product demo, voiceover only.

### The Green ORB Rule
The Diaphragmatic breathing bubble (SCREEN-BUBBLE — green pulsing orb) is the single most powerful visual in all app promos. It must appear in every video for minimum 4–5 seconds. It IS the product experience before the download.
