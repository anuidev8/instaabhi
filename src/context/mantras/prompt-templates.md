# School of Mantras Prompt Templates

These templates support the app's full-thumbnail workflow while respecting the guide's composition direction:

- Deity on the left 40-55%
- Three-line text block baked into the image on the right
- Small `SCHOOL OF MANTRAS` label top-left
- Dark background, dramatic aura, high contrast
- Deity iconography and aura must stay faithful to the reference guide, not genericized across deities

## Template A - 2026 Success Winner

Use for: `Ganesha`, `Lakshmi`, abundance-led videos

Formula:
`monumental deity portrait on left + warm amber-gold aura + bold right-side text block with year/benefit + action promise + sacred badge`

## Template B - Shield & Protection

Use for: `Shiva`, `Hanuman`, `Durga`, `Kali`

Formula:
`protective deity on left + Shiva electric blue OR Hanuman fiery orange OR Durga deep violet OR Kali crimson aura + strong right-side headline block + defensive action phrase + ritual badge`

## Template C - Healing Stack

Use for: `Medicine Buddha`, `Hanuman`, healing-led videos

Formula:
`compassionate healing deity on left + deity-faithful healing aura (Medicine Buddha deep blue-gold or Hanuman fiery protection energy) blended with healing calm + healing-led right-side copy block + small mantra badge`

## Template D - Love & Peace

Use for: `Krishna`, `Lakshmi`

Formula:
`emotionally resonant deity face on left + pink/blue devotional aura + high-contrast right-side love/peace copy block`

## Template E - Fierce Transformation

Use for: `Shiva`, `Kali`, `Durga`

Formula:
`fierce divine face on left + deep crimson or fiery aura + strong right-side transformation/power copy block`

## Template F - Wisdom & Clarity

Use for: `Saraswati`, `Ganesha`, `Ram`

Formula:
`wise regal deity on left + ivory or gold aura + elegant right-side knowledge/clarity text block`

## Template G - Close-Crop Face Dominance

Use for: any deity when expression is the strongest click driver

Formula:
`tight left-side face crop with intense eyes + oversized aura + clean dark right half with the full three-line text block`

## Variant Directives

Create 2 variants per request:

1. Closest to the proven channel winner and safest CTR layout.
2. Stronger aura, stronger contrast, slightly more cinematic drama.
