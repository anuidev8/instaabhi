# School of Mantras Prompt Templates (v4.2 ‚Äî Cinematic Divine Gold)

Style: **"Cinematic Divine Gold"** ‚Äî Cinematic Hyperreal Devotional (Golden Aura Style)

## Design Philosophy
- **Deity creates emotion** ‚Äî close-up on left 40-45%, face + blessing hand
- **Text delivers the message** ‚Äî MAX 2-3 words ONLY on the right, one dominant phrase
- **Composition drives the click** ‚Äî divine energy effects, high contrast, scroll-stopping layout

## The 4 Visual Systems (ALL must be present)
1. **CINEMATIC LIGHTING** ‚Äî strong gold rim light, glow bloom, high contrast shadows ‚Üí Hollywood feel
2. **DIVINE ENERGY** ‚Äî radial aura halo (concentric rings), floating particles, warm gold/orange tones, fire energy ‚Üí spiritual feel
3. **MATERIAL REALISM** ‚Äî physically reflective gold textures, intricate jewelry detail, depth + shadow realism ‚Üí premium feel
4. **EMOTIONAL FACE** ‚Äî soft compassionate eyes, calm expression, human-like skin texture, slight idealization (perfect symmetry, divine beauty) ‚Üí connection ‚Üí click

## Universal Character Rules
- Human-like skin texture (not flat color), realistic lighting on skin
- Physically accurate gold materials (reflective, not painted)
- Emotion in the eyes (alive, conscious, divine presence)
- Slight idealization (perfect symmetry, divine beauty)
- NOT cartoon, NOT illustration, NOT traditional painting, NOT temple sculpture
- Character fidelity: NO mixing iconography from other deities

## Environment Rules
- Background: deep dark gradient with warm golden energy effects ‚Äî swirling fire, embers, sparks, light flares
- Aura: dramatic golden concentric sacred rings/halo behind deity, smooth and powerful
- Lighting: strong golden rim light, golden sparks and embers floating around halo
- Atmosphere: warm golden fire/energy swirls, floating sparks, subtle mist at base, soft bokeh
- Hook text: subtle warm golden glow/highlight accent behind the hook word

## Variant Directives

Create 2 variants per request:

1. Proven winner layout with dramatic golden sacred ring halo, warm golden fire energy in background, hook text glowing with subtle warm highlight. Clean and balanced but visually alive.
2. Intensified aura ‚Äî bigger concentric golden rings, more golden sparks and embers, stronger fire energy swirls, bolder cinematic drama. Hook text has stronger golden glow accent. Maximum divine energy.

## What to Avoid in Every Prompt

- No clutter, no multiple props ‚Äî background effects should add energy NOT chaos
- No glowing text overload ‚Äî text has subtle warm highlight, not neon
- No complex storytelling elements
- No mixing symbolic meanings across deities
- No badges, logos, watermarks, or channel tags
- No props or objects in the text area (subtle warm glow bleed is OK)
- No center composition, no deity on right
- No more than 2-3 words of text
- No flat/plain backgrounds ‚Äî always include golden energy effects
